news card v2 — metadata, pills, category shaders

Two color systems that never collide: priority keeps the tier column / stamp / track (locked decision — news is always low-tier gray there), while category gets everything news owns — a sub-16%-alpha gradient shader drifting under the content, the masthead dot, and the pills. New wire metadata this requires from the backend: source ("NDTV"), category (politics/tech/sports/business/world), publishedAt — all derivable in the rss poller (per-feed config + RSS <category> tags + pubDate).
