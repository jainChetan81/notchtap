4 — cmux notification, full flow

Start idle (hover it — weather peek). Click "Notification arrives" — it auto-expands (full manifest), auto-retracts to compact halfway through, then rotates back to idle. 6 — option: native-notch resting state (hide-when-idle)

A config option, not a new design: everything works exactly as above, but the resting state is the bare native notch (section 1) instead of the time+dots rail. Promotions, event expansions, hover reveals, and rotate-out all behave the same — the card simply returns to invisible-native when there's nothing to say, instead of to the rail. Hovering the bare notch still opens the weather peek / football compact, so nothing is discoverable-only-by-accident: the rail is ambience, the bare notch is for people who want the screen back.
