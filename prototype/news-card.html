News card — shipped look + meta, cmux lifecycle

The compact card is the shipped news card, screenshot-faithful — masthead (category dot + source + hairline), headline, WIRE stamp, category + age pills, news-shade glow, track slider, ⌃⇧N MORE hint. One addition: published-time meta at the right end of the pills row. The expanded state drops the cramped 3-column manifest for a full-width summary + one inline meta row. And the card now runs the cmux lifecycle from prototype/notch-states.html: auto-expand on arrival, auto-retract to compact halfway through the window, rotate out — hover and ⌃⇧N toggle compact↔expanded at any point.

Design tokens (.cat-*, masthead, pills, news-shade) are copied verbatim from src/styles.css; lifecycle mechanics are copied from the notch-states cmux demo, so this page shows the news card as a pure content-swap on the shared template.
