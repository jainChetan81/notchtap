0 · The new lifecycle — every item enters expanded, retracts at half, slider advances

Plan 033: every promotion starts expanded regardless of priority, auto-retracts at half its rotation window, then rotates on — total turn length unchanged from before. The track is now a queue slider (batch semantics): dim = consumed, bright = current.

1 · Celebrations (shipped) — goal = burst + ring + ripple, red-card strobe

Plan 023's confetti burst + expanding ring, plus plan 032's three staggered accent ripples (your A+B pick). Red-card keeps its hard two-step strobe. Replay each.

2 · Priority — the chip is gone, the accent edge carries it

Plan 032: L1/M2/H3 deleted. Priority now reads from the 3px accent edge on the left, the stamp, and the slider's lit colour. Body copy is brighter/larger (0.74 alpha, 12px).

3 · Expanded manifests — markdown bodies, subtitle + detail cells

Bodies render inline markdown (`` `code` `` in accent mono, **bold**, *italic*, line breaks) in both the compact body and the brighter Message cell. Plan 035: the cmux/Claude-Code manifest gains a Subtitle cell and label/value detail cells (plain text, never markdown).

4 · News cards — unchanged skins, new chrome

Same six category shaders, masthead, and pills — sitting in the new chip-less compact row with the queue slider.

5 · Idle — clock-only vs the status rail

Plan 034: when football/news is on, a match is live, items are queued, or the engine is paused, the idle card widens to 460px and shows status chips (pulsing live dot, News gate, queue depth). When there's nothing to report it stays the plain 270px clock.

6 · Queue slider edge cases

Segment count caps at 10; beyond that the index maps proportionally (shown: 8 of 25). Plan 038 (shipped): skipping/rotating out a Recurring item no longer counts as "done" — both requeue arms are guarded, so a cycling live-match card can't over-advance the slider.

7 · Live-match scorecard (plan 042) — collapsed Clock + per-side Cards

Opt-in (espn_live_card) — a live match's collapsed card gains Clock and per-side Cards detail cells, same detail-label/detail-value classes as the expanded manifest, rendered inline under the body instead of behind ⌃⇧N. Option B: the card is never pinned open — it still rotates and collapses like any other item.
